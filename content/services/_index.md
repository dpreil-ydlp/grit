## Who This Is For

**Perfect for founders who:**
- Have 2-4 employees and feeling operational pain
- Generate $500K-$3M ARR (proven product-market fit)
- Spend 50%+ of time on operations vs. core work
- Are first-time founders without existing playbook
- Want to build something that lasts

**Not right for you if:**
- You're pre-revenue or just getting started
- You prefer to figure everything out yourself
- You're not open to modern tools and AI integration
- You want quick fixes instead of systematic transformation
