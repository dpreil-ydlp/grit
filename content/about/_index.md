## My Story

{{< raw >}}
<div class="story-section">
{{< /raw >}}

I know what it's like to be the founder who can't take a vacation because everything falls apart when you're gone.

I built and scaled a real estate investment platform from the ground up, dealing with the complexity of dual operational pipelines—managing deal flow AND investor relationships simultaneously. Picture this: every day managing property acquisitions, due diligence, financing negotiations, while also handling investor communications, compliance requirements, and fund distributions.

By the time we hit 4 employees, I was working 80-hour weeks and still dropping balls. Everything lived in my head. I was the bottleneck for every decision, every process, every new hire. Sound familiar?

The breaking point came when I realized I was afraid to take a single day off because I wasn't sure the business would run without me. That's when I knew something had to change.

I started studying how enterprise companies handle operational complexity and discovered something powerful: **you don't need 50 employees to have sophisticated operations**. You just need the right systems and the right people in the right roles.

{{< raw >}}
</div>
{{< /raw >}}

{{< raw >}}
<div class="highlight-box">
  <h3>The Breakthrough Moment</h3>
  <p>"I learned that great operations isn't about more process—it's about the right systems and the right people."</p>
</div>
{{< /raw >}}

After successfully scaling our platform to 6 employees with clear accountability, streamlined processes, and personality-matched team members, I had my "aha" moment. The same enterprise-level people strategies that worked for Fortune 500 companies could be adapted for small, growing teams.

We went from chaos to clarity. From reactive firefighting to proactive planning. From me being the bottleneck to having a team that could run independently.

Most importantly, we built something sustainable that supported our families and created real opportunities for our team members.

## My Mission

{{< raw >}}
<div class="story-section">
{{< /raw >}}

Helping founders build sustainable businesses that support their families and create opportunities for others. It's not just about growth—it's about building something that lasts.

I've seen too many founders burn out from operational chaos. I've watched brilliant people with game-changing ideas get stuck in the weeds of day-to-day operations instead of focusing on what they do best.

That's why I created Grit GrowthOps and the GRIT Framework. To give founders like you the operational foundation that enterprise companies take for granted, but adapted for the speed and flexibility that small teams need.
